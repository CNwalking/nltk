"Three months after the Nov. 13 attacks in Paris, a blocklong wall near two of the cafes targeted by terrorists has become Le Mur de l'Amour, or the Wall of Love. It's an initiative by residents of the 10th Arrondissement in Paris, who invited street and graffiti artists to respond to the attacks, doubling as an exercise in healing.
